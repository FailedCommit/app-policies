Welcome to Apprec8! This Privacy Policy explains how Apprec8 ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when you use our mobile application, Apprec8 (the "App").

The email address you provide is used exclusively for the following purposes:

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your email address with third parties for their marketing purposes.

  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the App.
  • Protect the personal safety of users of the App or the public.

Your email address is processed by Google's Firebase Authentication Service solely for the purpose of managing your login and account authentication on our behalf. Firebase Authentication Service is bound by confidentiality obligations and does not use your email for other purposes.

We will retain your email address for as long as your account remains active in our App. If you choose to delete your account, your email address will be deleted from our active systems in accordance with our Data Deletion Policy and applicable legal requirements.
